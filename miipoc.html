MIIPOC

Revitalizing the non-profit organization

Overview

The Mentoring Initiative for Indigenous & Planners of Colour (MIIPOC) is a non-profit organization that seeks to eliminate barriers faced by Indigenous and people of colour in the field of planning.

For my field placement as a postgraduate student at Centennial College’s Interactive Media Management, I redesigned the organization’s logo and provided them with branding guidelines. I also built mockups for a potential redesign of their website.
