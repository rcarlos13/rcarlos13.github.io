DRSHelper

Making gamers stop dying at that one boss

Overview

Picture this:

  • You’re playing an online game.
  • You’re in a dungeon fighting Boss #5 with 47 other people.
  • You and 30 other rookies die because the fight is really hard.
  • The rest of the team die because they couldn’t kill the boss in time.
  • All of you get kicked out of the dungeon and must fight Bosses #1–4 again (all of whom are also difficult).
  • But your group disbands instead because they’ve been doing this for over 3 hours.
  • You wish you can get more practice with Boss #5 so you won’t die next time.

This project is exactly that: an online tool that has helped dozens of players understand how to beat a notoriously difficult raid boss in the MMORPG Final Fantasy XIV. (“Have you heard of the critically acclaimed MMORPG Final Fan—”)
