About Me

I’ve been improving people’s experiences since before I even knew what user experience was. I strive to help others and make a difference, and I’m committed to putting a smile on everyone’s faces.

Through working as a Product Designer, I’m able to touch the lives of others by creating accessible, user-centric, interactive experiences. Knowing that the smallest decisions I make can really have a significant impact on improving people’s lives leaves me content at the end of the day.

When I’m not working on a project, I’m usually gaming on my Switch or PS5, reading fantasy novels, or spending too much money on concert tickets.
